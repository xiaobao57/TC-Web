Our helping hand comes both in the form of physical resources (such as cash cards and blankets) and, very importantly, emotional support. Ultimately, we want to give these individuals and their families the ability to stand back up and recover from the devastation of this disaster. From participating in distributions in the past, it has always been most touching to me to see the emotions that flow, both from us as the givers and from the disaster victims as receivers, as they share their stories with us and as we offer them our support.
