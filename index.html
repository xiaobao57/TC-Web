Tzu Chi Collegiate Association

We are the UT Austin chapter of TCCA, and we seek to build a community of compassionate students who work together to help those in need - regardless of race, religion, nationality, or otherwise.

Family


Spending all of our time giving to those in the community is fantastic, but we want to care for our volunteers' well-being. Our family system is intended to give you a group of people that you can rely on for fun, food, and ears to listen if you ever need one. We are grouped into four families each with their respective "parents" or adult volunteers who also help in the community and care for the family:

Service


TCCA Austin members are dedicated to the idea of giving from the heart. We believe if an individual wants to help others, a service organization should make it as easy as possible to give this person opportunities. In TCCA, there are no dues and no hour requirements--meaning you get access to our events at any time available in your schedule.

Social


We want to give our members the chance to meet with new people and bond with new friends. Thus, we host a number of larger socials aimed at giving these kinds of social opportunities where you can meet someone new or do something fun with an old friend!
